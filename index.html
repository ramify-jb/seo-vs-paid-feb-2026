Key Findings

Conversion definition: after landing on an article, the visitor navigated to a key page (offres, produits, tarifs...), a product page, the contact page, or the signup page. Some double-counting across categories; does not affect the comparison.
SEO converts better
Global CR: 2.7% SEO vs 1.7% Paid (+59% relative).
On 123 pages with events in both channels, SEO wins on 75 pages vs 45.
Engagement is nearly identical
On 137 pages with 100+ visitors:
Lower bounce rate: SEO 83 vs Paid 54.
Higher time on page: Paid 73 vs SEO 63.
SEO converters are more engaged
Events per converter: 7.0 SEO vs 5.9 Paid.
Total events: 8,420 SEO vs 6,002 Paid.
Paid drives more raw traffic
60,042 visitors (Paid) vs 45,053 (SEO).
But SEO delivers more conversions per visitor.
